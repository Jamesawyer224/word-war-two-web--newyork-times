The Story of World War II

1939: The Beginning

World War II begins with Germany's invasion of Poland on September 1st. The UK and France declare war on Germany.

1941: Pearl Harbor

The United States enters the war after Japan's attack on Pearl Harbor on December 7th.

1944: D-Day

D-Day, the Allied invasion of Normandy, occurs on June 6th, marking a turning point in the war.

1945: The End

Germany surrenders on May 7th, and Japan surrenders on September 2nd, officially ending the war.

Key Locations of World War II

Battle of Stalingrad (1942-1943): A pivotal Soviet victory that marked the decline of the Axis in Europe.
